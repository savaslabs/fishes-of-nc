Authored by Bryn H. Tracy, Fred C. Rohde, Scott A. Smith, Jesse L. Bissette, and Gabriela M. Hogue, this book will serve as a companion to An Annotated Atlas of the Freshwater Fishes of North Carolina and NCFishes.com.

A Southern Gateways Guide

Just in time for the 2024 field season, this book will provide you with:

  • Detailed identification keys, updated taxonomy, and full-color images for all 258 described and undescribed species
  • Distributional maps for each species
  • Information on the 40 freshwater fish families and the ichthylogical history of North Carolina
  • An appendix that explains the meanings behind the scientific names
  • A glossary with color images describing the morphological characters that are used for identification
